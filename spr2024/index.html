New England Natural Language Processing (NENLP) is a meeting series for academic and industry NLP researchers that live and work in New England.

When is the NENLP 2024 meet up?
  • April 26, 2024, 10:00 am - 5:00 pm.
  • Venue: Sayles Hall, Providence, RI 02912
Schedule
Transportation/Parking
  • By train: MBTA/Amtrak trains travel directly from South Station (Boston) to Providence Station.
  • By car: Unfortunately, Brown does not have any any available designated lots, but there is plenty of street parking within a 10 min walk or less. Please be mindful of any parking signs.

Call for Submissions

We invite submissions for the NENLP 2024 meetup, a one-day event dedicated to exploring the latest developments in NLP research. We welcome submissions on all aspects of NLP. Some of them will be selected for oral presentations and the remaining will be presented as posters. We encourage submissions from rising researchers who are enrolled in graduate programs at universities located in the New England region.

Dates
  • Poster submission deadline: April 5, 2024
  • Notification deadline: April 12, 2024
  • Event date: April 26, 2024
Submission Guidelines
  • Submissions can be a work in progress or already published work. The overall length of the extended abstract should not exceed 500 words.
  • Titles and abstracts should be submitted through the link by the submission deadline.
Poster Presentations
  • Posters should be 4 feet wide x 3 feet tall.
  • Poster presenters will be assigned a designated time and space during the conference to present their work.
  • For setting up the posters, presenters should arrive at the presentation location at least 20 min before the presentation. Volunteers will provide stationery items required for presenters and help mount the posters.
  • Presenters should be prepared to discuss their work with conference attendees and answer questions.
Oral Presentations
  • The submissions will be reviewed and some of them will be selected for oral presentation.
